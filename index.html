About Kreawit

We are a group of Fullstack developers and Software development enthusiasts with a passion for innovation within IoT, AI, Mobile and Web Application Development. Our pages are not supposed to replace Stack Overflow but arise from our wish to share our wide experience in IoT, Mobile and Web application development. The pages will also provide a number of projects that can function as innovation kick starter for people starting development in the respective areas. We believe in knowledge sharing and hope that other developers with similar interests will contribute to our common knowledge base.
